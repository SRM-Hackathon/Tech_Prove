Training Scheme for SC and ST Farmers


Training Scheme for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Farmers launched by Government of Madhya Pradesh. The main objective behind introducing this scheme is the welfare of for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Farmers and improving farm productivity and improving livelihood level for sustainability lanes. Training Scheme for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Farmers makes farmer to aware with ample of agriculture technology to do so Madhya Pradesh Government organizes various training session and tour to inside or outside the state. All farmer residence of Madhya Pradesh state is eligible for this scheme or can get the benefit. The farmer who willing to get benefits of this plan ought to apply to Nearest Krishi Vistar Adhikari. It is required for farmer to increase their crop production and improve livelihood level

Required eligibility and conditions for applying Training Scheme for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Farmers:

1. Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes Farmers residence of Madhya Pradesh state are eligible under this scheme or can get benefit of this scheme

2. Kisan Mitra, Kisan Didi are eligible for the scheme
