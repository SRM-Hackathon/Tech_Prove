Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)


Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)
Launched: 8 April 2015
Main Objective: Financial support for growth of micro enterprises sector. Pradhan Mantri MUDRA (Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency) Yojana was launched with the purpose to provide funding to the non-corporate small business sector. Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY) is open and is available from all Bank branches across the country. The small businesses/startups or entrepreneurs can avail loans from Rs. 50 thousand to 10 Lakh to start/grow their business under the three, Shishu, Kishore and Tarun categories of the scheme. As per the official website of PMMY, 27344053 number of loans have been sanctioned under the scheme till 26 February 2016. The amount sanctioned has reached more than Rs. 1 Lakh Crore.
Official Website: http://www.mudra.org.in

The Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojna is known as MUDRA (micro-unit development and refinance agency) is the scheme introduced by the finance ministry on 8 April 2015. The purpose of MUDRA is to provide funding to the noncorporate small business, entrepreneur, proprietorship/partnership firms running a small manufacturing unit, shopkeeper, food vegetable sellers, hair cutting saloons, beauty parlors, transporters, truck operators, hawkers, co-operative or body of individuals, food service units, repair shops, machine operators, small industries, artisans, etc in rural area and urban will get funding up to 10 lakh. And also provide guidance, that how to operate a business in order to get more profit.
Benefits of Pradhan Mantri Mudra:
• Low-cost funding will be available to beneficiary those, who have a small business unit. Or low-interest rate on loan amount • PM Mudra Bank Yojana would help in facilitating micro-credit up to Rs 10 lakh to such small business owners • MUDRA would help in increasing the overall output and creating fewer jobs • There will be an increase in the enthusiasm of business unit, which would help in-country financial development • More jobs will be available and the unemployment rate will be decreased • For getting more profit and output guidance and skill will be provided under MUDRA • Under MUDRA loan will be available in Three category 1. Shishu– Loan up to ₹50,000 2. Kishore– Loan ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹5 lakh 3. Tarun– Loan above ₹5 lakh and below ₹10 lakh
