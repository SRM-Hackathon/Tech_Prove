Gaon Ki Beti Yojana (GKBY)


Someone truly said that the real definition of development of a country is its rural area. The more rural area is advanced, the more growing will be the economy of the country. But, in India rural India lags behind little bit. Reasons are many, but one common reason is women in rural areas are not developing, they are powerless because of illiteracy. And now no more of dominating women. Madhya Pradesh government came up with the scheme named Gaon Ki Beti Yojana under which the govt. will provide financial assistance to the girl child to undertake higher education. Due to this women will get empowered and can contribute in the development of rural area. With such a great initiative girl child can pursue their desirous course and can fly till great heights.

Benefits of Gaon Ki Beti Yojana:

Financial assistance of Rs. 5000: Under the scheme the girls from weaker economic background will also get chance to pursue higher education and will get scholarship of Rs. 5000 per year
Women empowerment: The scheme will indirectly empower the women by bringing them into main stream by educating them
Awareness about the rights: Educating women will make them aware about their rights and there wonâ€™t be any more injustice in the society with women
Eligibility for Gaon Ki Beti Yojana:

Only girls are eligible for the scheme

Must be the resident of Madhya Pradesh state
Should have completed education till her HSC
Village in which student is living should be more than 5 Km away from the city
Application form:
To apply for the scheme girl child has to take application form from the college itself and submit it to the principal of the college. Students can download the form from the following link DownloadFormsIndia_Govt_2976
