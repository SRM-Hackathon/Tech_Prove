Training Cum Production Center Scheme for ST/SC Students


Training Cum Production Center Scheme (TCPC) launched by the state Government of Madhya Pradesh for schedule tribes student in the state. The main purpose of the scheme is to provide technical training to least educated young men and women of Schedule Tribes for their self-employment. Regarding this, the government provides one year Vocational/Technical Training to the less educated candidate through Training cum Production Centers (TCPC). There are 9 Training cum Production Centers (TCPC) are operated in 9 districts of Tribal Sub-Plan Area in the State. The 9 districts centers are Jhabua, Barwani Rawati – Ratlam, Mandla, Mandla, Amarkantak – Anooppur, Sidhi, Narwali, Baihar, Balaghat. The government has sanctioned total no. of 420 seats are in these 9 Centers operated in the State. The government also provide Rs. 250/- to Boys and Rs. 260/- to girls is given every month as a scholarship to selected trainees. Raw material/equipment/tools for training are provided by the Institute. Guidance is also provided from time to time to Passed trainees to establish their self-employment. The training provided in this Training Cum Production Center Scheme (TCPC) are Carpenter, Sewing, Blacksmithy, Knitting, Meson, Doll Making, Brush Making, Sheet Metal, Work of Silk Industry, Mat Industry, Embroidery, Cane and Bamboo Furniture etc.

Benefits of Training Cum Production Center Scheme:
1. Government provide one year Vocational/Technical Training to the less educated candidate through Training cum Production Centers (TCPC)
2. The government also provides a scholarship of Rs. 250/- to the Boys and Rs. 260/- to the girls per month for the selected trainees
3. Raw material / equipment’s / tools for training are provided by the Institute
4. Guidance is also provided from time to time to Passed trainees to establish their self employment

Eligibility for Training cum Production Center Scheme:
1. Applicant should be resident of Madhya Pradesh state
2. All Scheduled caste and Scheduled Tribe candidates of Madhya Pradesh state are eligible for this Training Cum Production Center Scheme
How to apply for Training Cum Production Center Scheme:

1. Applicant can contact to the Collector of concerned district/District Assistant Commissioner, Tribal Development Department / District Organizers, Tribal Welfare Department / Manager of Institute
2. The Selections procedure are made through Institution’s Manager/District Officer
