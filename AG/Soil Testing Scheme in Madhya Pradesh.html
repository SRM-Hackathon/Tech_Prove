Soil Testing Scheme in Madhya Pradesh


Soil Testing Scheme launched by Government of Madhya Pradesh (Farmer Welfare and Agriculture Department). An objective of this scheme is to encourage farmer of state for adopting advanced farming techniques. The scheme provides soil testing and soil health card at a very cheaper rate for ex. For general category farmer Rs. 5 per sample and for SC/ST/BC category farmer Rs. 3 per sample. To provides such facility Madhya Pradesh government has started 75 laboratories across. state All farmer residence of Madhya Pradesh state is eligible under this scheme. The candidate who willing to get a benefit of this plan should apply to Nearest Laboratory. Madhya Pradesh Government has introduced several schemes for the benefit of the farmers this is one of them the prime initiative Benefits of Soil Testing Scheme in Madhya Pradesh:

Required eligibility and conditions applying for Soil Testing Scheme:

1. All farmer residence of Madhya Pradesh state are eligible under this scheme

Document required applying for Soil Testing Scheme:
1. Residence proof
2. Identity proof: Aadhar card
3. 7/12 of farm
4. Income certificate
5. Application form
