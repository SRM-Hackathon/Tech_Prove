Ground water conservation


Ground Water Conservation Scheme launched by Government of Madhya Pradesh (Farmer Welfare and Agriculture Department). Under this scheme Department of Agriculture constructs percolation tanks in all district of Madhya Pradesh state for ground water conservation, which results in an increase in ground water level. This scheme started by the state government with the objective of farmer welfare and increase in ground water level to do so government provides a fund of Rs. 5 lakhs to construct percolation tank. All residence farmer of Madhya Pradesh state is eligible under this scheme. The farmer who willing to get benefits of the scheme is not required to apply because indirectly benefits are provided by building percolation tank. Madhya Pradesh Government has introduced several schemes for the benefit of the farmers this is one of them the prime initiative Benefits of Ground Water Conservation Scheme in Madhya Pradesh:
• Ground Water Conservation Scheme helps farmer by making them wealthiest with a water source. Implementation of this scheme will lead to construction of percolation tank in all district of Madhya Pradesh state this process results conservation of water, increase in water level
• Water level in Nalkoop will increase
Required eligibility and conditions applying for Ground Water Conservation Scheme:

1. All farmer residence of Madhya Pradesh state will get benefits of this scheme once construction of percolation tanks
Document required applying for Ground Water Conservation Scheme:

1. No documents required implementation of the scheme is carried out by the Department of Agriculture

Application procedure:
1. Construction of percolation tank is done over government land so doesn’t take separate procedure of selecting farmer
2. Implementation of the scheme is carried out by the Department of Agriculture. Farmers are not required to apply because indirectly benefits are provided by building percolation tank
